About us

Sparrarp Islandhastar

Horse Riding School & Recreation Center

Sparrarp's Icelandic Horses is a business that offers people with different needs and wishes to ride and socialize with Icelandic horses - who are well trained to be ridden with Natural Horsemanship.
It is with natural horse management where all the training is based from the ground up which involves communicating, handling and training the horses in a way that is natural for the horse.
The result of this is a self-supporting horse. A partner with his own influence who willingly does his best, because it has developed joy in the work.
On the farm we have 17 horses.
As our facility also has a purpose built indoor riding arena , it is possible to book for your own events or to hold your own courses.
Riding with our horses together in the many acres surrounding the school will give you the competence to become a very experienced rider and to give you many hours of enjoyment - plus a certian amount of fitness
Phillip is the owner and responsible for the horses' health and well-being, as well as maintenance and care of the farm.
