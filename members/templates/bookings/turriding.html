Turridning

A Great Adventure & Experience

Sparrarp's Icelandic horses are located near Hästveda in Hässleholm municipality.
All the horses available are suitable tour horses all with different characters. It is important that each rider finds the right horse to ride,
based on previous experience
Suitable clothes for riding should be comfortable, loose trousers without seams so as not to rub and sturdy shoes.
If rain is suspected, a raincoat that is easy to carry in case of a shower
The horses are available for a short period of time, from the beginning of the year to the end of the year.
The price is from 200 kr per horse per day.
